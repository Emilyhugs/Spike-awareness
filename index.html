What is spiking?

Spiking is when someone secretly puts a drug or alcohol in your drink without you knowing. The goal is usually to make you feel drunk, dizzy, or out of control, often so they can steal from you or assault you. Spiking can also happen through needles, where someone injects a drug into you without your consent.

Did you know that...

  • 11% of UK adults report being spiked at any point in their lives
  • The most commonly used drugs to spike drinks are: Rohypnol, GHB and Ketamine

What are the Symptoms of Spiking?

Nausea or vomiting

If you or your friend suddenly feel nauseous, get help immediately

Confusion

If you or your friend suddenly feel confused, get help immediately

Disorientation

If you or your friend suddenly feel disorientated, get help immediately

Memory Loss

If you or your friend have memory loss, get help immediately
